I make Pokemon mods for twitch streamers sometimes. If you would like to comission me, feel free to contact me and we can get in touch. Most of the money I make from this is being put towards saving for when I become a broke graduate student, so rest assured it's for a good cause. Pricing may vary but if I take an interest in your project and you're a smaller creator I will be fine taking a bit of lower pay so it remains affordable.

Alternatively, if you're looking to start modding yourself, feel free to message me over discord or check out the PRET repositories on GitHub, and I can help find a good starting point based on your goals. You can also check out my decompilation guide (WIP) for how to get started translating assembly into c for any game!

I'm most well known for my Heart Gold and Soul Silver Map Randomizer, but I also have a large body of work you can check out here. Most of my mods are based on the generation 4 PRET repositories, which I'm both contribute to and maintain.
